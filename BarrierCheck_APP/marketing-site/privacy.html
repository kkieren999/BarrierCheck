Privacy Policy

Starter draft: have this privacy policy reviewed before public launch.

Information collected

BarrierCheck may collect account details, inspector profile details, company details, inspection records, client details, property details, notes and evidence photos.

How information is used

Information is used to provide the software, save inspections, generate reports, support accounts and manage billing.

Security

BarrierCheck uses cloud services such as Firebase and Stripe. Users should keep account passwords secure and only upload information they are authorised to handle.
